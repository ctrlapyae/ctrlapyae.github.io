Advertisement Policy

MRates - Advertising & Credit System Information

This Advertisement Policy explains how advertisements work in the MRates app, our credit-based ad removal system, and your choices regarding ads. By using the app, you acknowledge and agree to this policy.

MRates uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. This helps keep the app free while providing valuable services.

Types of Advertisements

MRates displays the following types of advertisements through Google AdMob:

Banner Ads
Small ads displayed at the top or bottom of screens
Interstitial Ads
Full-screen ads shown between app sections
Rewarded Ads
Optional ads that reward users with credits
Native Ads
Integrated ads matching app's design

Ad Frequency: Ads are shown at reasonable intervals to maintain user experience. We implement ad cooldowns to prevent excessive advertising.

Ad Content: All ads are served through Google AdMob and comply with Google's advertising policies. We do not control specific ad content.

Credit System & Ad Removal

MRates offers a credit-based system that allows users to temporarily remove ads by earning or spending credits.

How Credits Work
Earning Credits
Watch optional rewarded ads to earn credits (1 credit per ad)
Spending Credits
Use credits to purchase temporary ad-free periods
Credit Balance
Credits are stored locally on your device
Ad-Free Periods
Temporary periods when no ads are shown

Ad-Free Packages

Package Duration Cost (Credits) Features
Daily 1 Day 10 Credits Remove all ads for 24 hours
Weekly 7 Days 69 Credits Remove all ads for 1 week
Monthly 30 Days 290 Credits Remove all ads for 1 month

Important Notes:

  • Ad-free periods are temporary and will expire automatically
  • Credits have no monetary value and cannot be exchanged for cash
  • Ad-free status is device-specific and not transferable
  • Clearing app data will reset credits and ad-free status

User Privacy & Data Collection

Advertisement delivery involves limited data collection through Google AdMob:

Data Collected by AdMob:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data for ad performance

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Precise location data
  • Contact information
  • Sensitive personal data

For more details on Google's data practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I permanently remove ads?

A: The app offers temporary ad removal through the credit system. There is no permanent ad removal option at this time.

Q: What happens if I clear app data?

A: Clearing app data will reset your credits and any active ad-free periods. This data is stored locally on your device.

Q: Are credits transferable between devices?

A: No, credits and ad-free status are device-specific and cannot be transferred.

Q: How can I report inappropriate ads?

A: If you encounter inappropriate ads, please contact us with details. You can also report directly through Google AdMob.
